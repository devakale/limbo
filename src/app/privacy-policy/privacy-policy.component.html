Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • User Profiles: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, password, and profile picture.
  • Expert Profiles: When you create an expert profile, we collect your name, email address, bio, expertise, credentials, and other information relevant to your expertise.
  • Session Data: When you book a session, we collect the date, time, duration, and topic of the session.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Services: We use information to provide our services, including connecting experts and seekers, processing payments, and communicating with users.
  • To Facilitate Connections: We use information to facilitate connections between experts and seekers, including matching seekers with relevant experts.
  • To Communicate with Users: We use email addresses to communicate with users, including sending notifications, updates, and marketing emails.
  • To Analyze Usage: We use data to analyze usage patterns and improve the user experience.

Data Protection

We take the following measures to protect user data:

  • Encryption: We use encryption to protect data transmission.
  • Secure Servers: We store data on secure servers.
  • Access Controls: We implement access controls and authentication measures to ensure only authorized personnel can access user data.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Aggregated Data: We may share aggregated, anonymized data for research or marketing purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may share information in response to legal requirements, such as court orders or subpoenas.

User Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Users can access and update their personal information.
  • Right to Delete: Users can delete their account and data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Users can opt-out of marketing communications.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and tracking pixels to:

  • Monitor Marketing Efforts: We use tracking pixels to monitor marketing efforts and improve advertising.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We will notify users of changes to this policy, and changes will be effective immediately.
